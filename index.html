About the artist

Katerina is an artist whose love for stories began in childhood. Growing up with cartoons and worlds like Alice in Wonderland The NeverEnding Story and The Lord of the Rings inspired her to create stories of her own through illustration. After many years working in graphic design she decided to return to what she had always enjoyed most. She works in two different visual styles: clean line art with little or no texture and richly textured illustrations with depth and detail. Moving between these approaches allows her to simplify or enrich the visuals depending on the story she wants to tell. She is available for brand collaborations and custom illustration commissions.
