About the artist

Katerina is an artist whose love for stories began in childhood. Growing up with cartoons and worlds like The NeverEnding Story, Alice in Wonderland, and The Lord of the Rings inspired her to create worlds of her own through illustration. After many years working in graphic design, she decided to return to what had always inspired her the most.

She works in two different visual styles: clean line art with little or no texture and richly textured illustrations with depth and detail. Moving between these approaches allows her to combine these two sides of her creativity and make each project personal.

Katerina is available for brand collaborations and custom illustration commissions.
